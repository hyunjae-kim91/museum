Ichiro had a remarkable 2001 season, accumulating a rookie-record 242 hits, the most by any MLB player since 1930. Ichiro also had a perennial Gold Glove fielding led Safeco's right field to be dubbed 'Area 51'. Ichiro would hit .350 and steal 56 bases and was the first player to record this feat since Jackie Robinson in 1949.
Rookie bat from 2001 shows light use with a few ball marks, stitch impressions along with white leather scuffs on the right and back barrel. Knob stamped 51A and is uncracked. 33.5 inches and 31.4 oz. PSA Graded GU 7.
